Overview

I'm a data journalist and graphics editor with 15 years of experience at the intersection of reporting, data visualization and computer science. My specialty is researching and producing print and interactive graphics based on large datasets.

My favorite projects involve partnering with scientists and researchers to visualize their discoveries in creative and arresting ways, or even allowing readers to participate in IRB-approved studies. While I am capable of executing every stage of a project, I prefer to manage a small team of colleagues and collaborators.

I am the author of RaphaëlJS: Graphics and Visualization on the Web and the coauthor of two peer-reviewed papers.
